Who We Are

Shaping Lives Since 1998

Established in Kadapa, Balavikas has been a pioneer in quality English-medium education, focusing on comprehensive academic growth and ethical values.

Our Proud History

Balavikas English Medium High School was founded with a dream of delivering outstanding education that combines modern global competencies with deep-rooted moral values. Over the past 25+ years, we have grown into one of Kadapa's most trusted institutions, guided by experienced educators and a student-first philosophy.

Our Vision

To cultivate self-reliant, ethical, and knowledgeable global citizens ready to contribute positively to society.

Our Mission

To provide a stimulating learning environment that blends digital technology, top-tier academic rigor, and co-curricular programs.

Learning Pathways

Academic Structure & Facilities

Designed to spark intellectual curiosity, our modern curriculum supports student progression across distinct growth milestones.

Grades 1 to 5

Primary Wing

Strong fundamentals in English literacy, mathematical operations, and environment consciousness. Interactive activity-based learning sessions.

  • Focus on Language Medium (English/Telugu)
  • Arithmetic & Visual Logic
  • Art & Craft Integrations
Grades 6 to 8

Middle Wing

Introduction to advanced concepts in science disciplines, historical structures, and grammar mechanics. Structured lab visits are introduced.

  • Integrated Sciences & ICT
  • Third Language (Hindi)
  • Problem Solving Clubs
Grades 9 to 10

Secondary Wing (SSC)

Specialized preparations for SSC Public Examinations. Focus on scientific inquiry, algebraic expressions, and social theories.

  • 100% Board Pass Preparation
  • Computer Programming Labs
  • Weekly Mock Examinations

State-of-the-Art Facilities

Balavikas provides students with top-grade learning infrastructure that facilitates experimentation, exploration, and creative reasoning. We believe that classroom learning is enhanced when coupled with practical validation.

Digital Smart Classrooms
Every teaching room is equipped with high-definition digital smart boards and audio setups, enabling visual explanations and interactive multi-media lessons.
Composite Science Laboratory
Fully equipped chemical reagents, physical apparatus (microscopes, lenses), and biological charts to allow students to observe principles firsthand.
ICT Computer Lab
Our modern computer lab has individual student terminals with internet connectivity, focusing on basic office tools, logic building, and introductory coding.
